Our Mission

Here at EAT, our goal is to deliver the ultimate gluten-free products. We are excited about the growing demand for gluten-free, and are eager to prove that no gluten does not have to equal no taste. After all, food is food, and food should be enjoyed - gluten or no gluten.

Our Team

Chef John Smith is an internationally acclaimed pastry and restaurant chef and one of the pioneers of gluten-free. Together with his team, he has created an extensive selection of the finest gluten-free dishes. All that's left? For you to EAT!

&bakery

At the heart of EAT is our award winning bakery. Tired of the crumbly and dry gluten free pastries that taste more like sand than pastries? Fear no more. Our talented kitchen team has put together the finest assortment of pastries - so good, you'll wonder if there's secretly gluten inside.


(But rest assured, there isn't.)
